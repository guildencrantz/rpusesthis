Who are you?

My name is Tom Sather, and I am Senior Director of Research at Return Path. I run our content marketing program which includes things like research papers, ebooks, and webinars to demonstrate Return Path’s expertise in email.

My iPhone 5s is essentially a part of my body at this point, so a lot of my productivity tools are mobile apps. I also use a 15" MacBook Pro with Retina display for all design work and data analysis since it’s extremely capable of large tasks that PCs struggle with.

What apps do you use?

Mail

  • Apple Mail
  • Gmail

Calendar

  • Tempo – intelligent calendar using technology from SRI

Productivity

On my iPhone, I have a folder specifically called “Get Sh*t Done” which includes:

  • ActionMethod – Adobe bought this so I’m afraid they will ignore it, but it’s great for getting quick ideas and to-dos down. Definitely my #1 used app for managing tasks
  • Reminders
  • TaskBox Pro
  • SmartSheet – mainly for work things. Love that it has a calendar feature which allows me to subscribe and sync across many calendars like Google or Outlook.
  • Any.do
  • Trello
  • TIME Planner
  • OmniFocus – great for contextual reminders of things I need to do

I also have a folder for ideation apps (specifically apps to jot ideas down):

  • Moleskine Journal
  • MindMeister
  • Evernote – most used since I can take photos of handwritten notes, etc.

Travel

  • BestOf
  • United Airlines
  • Hipmunk
  • Jetsetter
  • Airbnb
  • Uber
  • App in the Air
  • GateGuru
  • Airport Car Rental
  • Triposo

Cloud Storage (both mobile and desktop)

  • Google Drive

Social Media
