About

What is this?

RP Uses This is a collection of profiles of the diverse members of the Return Path team. The question we're trying to answer: what do you use to get the job done?

Who made this?

Andy Smith (UX Designer) designed and created the site, and Hoon Park (Support Ops Manager) collected and edited the profiles. This was created during the 2014 Q1 RP Hackathon.

Where did the idea come from?

There was a group of people who were working on coming up with some standards or suggestions to the rest of the company on tools they could use to increase productivity. We thought a site like this might be a fascinating look into how different people in the company get stuff done both at work and at home.

How was it made?

The site uses the Middleman static site generator and is hosted on GitHub Pages. We used SASS as our CSS preprocessor and a stripped down version of Twitter Bootstrap for SASS.

Can I write a profile?

Yes! Feel free to send one to Hoon and he will make sure it gets on the site. Don't feel like you have to add a bunch of links. He will let you know if he needs any additional information.
